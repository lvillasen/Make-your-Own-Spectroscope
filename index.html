Introduction

This app consists of three parts.

The first part calculates the diffraction angles for a beam of light incident with a given angle on a difraction grating.

The second part ilustrates how to construct a simple high-resolution spectroscope by using a tube and a CD or DVD.

The third part allows the capture of the spectra with a webcam and its calibration.
